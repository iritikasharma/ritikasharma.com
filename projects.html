Flagship Research

Quantum Dot Synthesis & Optical Characterisation

Investigating the size-dependent optical properties of semiconductor quantum dots synthesised via colloidal chemistry. The project focuses on tuning emission wavelengths for bioimaging applications and developing rapid characterisation protocols using UV-Vis and fluorescence spectroscopy. Current phase involves systematic surface passivation studies to improve photoluminescence quantum yield.
