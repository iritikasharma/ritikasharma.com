The Process

Art born from data

Every piece starts in the lab — with real experimental data, microscope images, or published research. I translate the raw material of science into visual language that communicates without requiring a PhD to appreciate.

Science art isn't decoration. It's a communication tool, a teaching aid, and an invitation for the curious.

01
Research & Data Gathering
Understanding the science deeply before any visual decisions are made.
02
Concept Development
Finding the visual metaphor that makes the science click.
03
Design & Iteration
Building in Illustrator or Figma, testing legibility at every step.
04
Accuracy Review
Every element checked against the source material for scientific integrity.
